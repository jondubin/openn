Welcome to Openn

Pretty soon, you'll have access to the largest database of course grade distributions available at the University of Pennsylvania. To get started, we ask that you contribute to the anonymous database by importing your own grade history. You can do this with our Chrome extension by following the directions below.

So far, {{numUsers}} students have imported their grades from Penn InTouch. Importing your grades is completely anonymous, and we do not collect any identifying information, aside from your past course history, performance, graduation year, and school. Feel free to check out the source code on GitHub if you are interested in how the extension functions. We believe in openness and transparency above all, and we hope you find this project useful.

- You'll need Google Chrome to set up your Openn account. After setting up your account, you can use your browser of choice. Install our Chrome extension, available here.

- Go to Penn Intouch and login. On the sidebar, expand the "Academic records" section and go to "View grades."

- On the "View grades" page, click on the large "Import grades into Openn" button at the top. Wait for the import process to finish. When it completes, the button will change from green to blue.

- When the import finishes, you can close Penn InTouch and can even uninstall the Openn Chrome extension. Return to Openn and refresh the page. You now have access to the complete database of distributions as well as powerful personal analytics. Enjoy.
